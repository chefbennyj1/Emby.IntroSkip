Detection Statistics

Statistical overview of detection successes for TV Shows and Seasons.

In order to generate statistics, the IntroSkip Statistics Task must be run, under Scheduled Tasks

Some episodes are broadcast without title sequences, or only include a short title screen which would not be considered a title sequence.
This should be taken into consideration when reading detection statistic data.

Disabling will show only statistics for TV Shows and Seasons that do not have 100% detection success
Enabling will show statistical information for all TV Shows and Seasons.
