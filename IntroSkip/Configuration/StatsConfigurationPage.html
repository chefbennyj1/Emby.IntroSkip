IntroSkip Detection Statistics

This Page allows you to run statistics on the detection success of all your TV Shows and seasons, allowing you to investigate Seasons which have a problem with their episodes.

Disabling will show only statistics for TV Shows and Seasons that do not have 100% detection success
Enabling will show statistics for all TV Shows and Seasons (Good and Bad)
